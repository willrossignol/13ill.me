Vinyl collection

I've always enjoyed music as it's helped me to get through som hard times in my life.

When I got my first car, I started buying some CDs that I could play in my car, even if I had a streaming service I could find that music on. I liked the idea of physical media and had grown up listening to CDs with my mom and dad during car rides (I remember clearly listening to Alexandre Poulin with my mom and AC/DC with my dad).

After a while, I started getting into vinyls. I was buying vinyls even before I had a record player. I liked to have these big square cover arts displayed in my room. Nowadays, I've always got a record ready to go in my record player with its cover art on display in my living room.
