### Beware the attention economy

:::
This is a rant I wrote in my notes app. 
I wrote this while listenting to Getting Killed by Geese. There was something about the song that made all of this just flow out.
I've been preoccupied by the attention economy for a while, trying to be more conscious about how I use technology. 
I've tried deleting apps from my phone, or blocking them on a schedule, but there's always a way to use those platforms.
I would either just use a work computer, or disable the roadblocks that I setup myself.
It's a constant battle, but I think it's one worth fighting.
:::

~

:::
We’ve been told to make budgets for our finances
Because in this society, money is king
:::

:::
But what about Time
Have we forgotten the importance that Time has in our lives
How Time just slips away, without you even noticing it
We have a finite amount of Time, and this amount is going down every second, but we don’t seem to put as much emphasis on making sure we use our Time carefully
:::

:::
You should know by now that you’re being robbed
Almost every app on your phone is designed to steal your precious Time
Taking advantage of your psychological shortcomings as a human being, and exploiting you
:::

:::
They’re showing you images of a life you could live
If only you were to pack your bag and go
It’s just much easier to lay around on the couch and see all this stuff through a 2x6 inch piece of glass
:::

:::
They’re robbing you of your attention by exploiting your feelings
Showing you stuff that will make you angry so you engage with it
Showing you stuff that will make you even more depressed so you keep scrolling
Showing you stuff that will destroy you, so you keep using their digital methamphetamine
:::

:::
Put it down
Gain control
Think about what you’re doing
Be bored
Understand where you’re headed
What you want in this life
This is your only one
Don’t waste it on the capitalist’s booby trap
:::

:::
Make a budget for your attention
Be intentional about where you put your energy
:::
