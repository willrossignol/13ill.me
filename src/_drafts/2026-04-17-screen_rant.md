title: Screen Rant
author: William Rossignol
---

This is an excerpt from my Notes app. I think I was just doomscrolling on Instagram and 
got really frustrated with myself and the platforms that keep us addicted to our phones.

~

:::
“Stop doing these three things”
“Five meals you need to make”
“Four places you have to visit”
:::

I’m tired of hearing about everything 

:::
Stop giving me recommendations,
Just so I stay on your platform
:::

:::
Let me experience my life
Let me figure out what’s good and what’s bad
:::

~

I’m living through a screen

:::
My work happens sat at a desk in front of a screen
I talk to my coworkers through my screen
:::

:::
The people that I’ve met, I keep up with them through the screen
I see them once every couple of weeks
But everyday on the screen, they don’t even know I see them
:::

:::
My closest friends, I talk to every week through a screen
I see them in person every couple months
:::

:::
My leisure time is done through bigger screens
A movie, some content, couple of games, all consumed through a screen
I don’t know any better
It’s what I’m used to
It’s what’s easy
:::

This piece of glass has taken so much from me

The same piece of glass on which I’m typing this on

~

:::
I’ve been robbed
And I’ve kept on giving to this thief
They’ve taken everything from me
I don’t know who I am
:::
